Executive summaryResumen ejecutivo

Across 10 active flower rooms at Jolo Can, the 2-week window ending 21 Apr 2026 shows a facility where climate hardware is reporting cleanly — all rooms have full TEROS-15 + ATMOS-14 coverage — but where substrate temperatures run warm facility-wide (median 77.0 °F with Flower 6 and Flower 8 above 80 °F), relative humidity drifts 5 – 10 points above the mid-flower 55 – 65 % target in the hotter rooms, and pore EC sits below the 6 – 8 mS/cm bulking band in 6 of 10 rooms. VPD is the facility's strongest performer — sitting in or near the bulking band of 1.0 – 1.2 kPa across the board. Dryback behavior is healthy (median 17 – 32 % across rooms) with no runaway zones. Flower 5 stands out with a 93 %-peak VWC reading that warrants a zone-level recheck. Full breakdown and recommendations below.
